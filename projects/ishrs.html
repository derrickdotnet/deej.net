International Society of Hair Restoration Surgeons

Role: UX/UI Designer & Developer

Software: WordPress w/Elementor

Results

  • Improved the user experience by updating the interface and site layout (information architecture)
  • In one year, traffic increased by over 22%.

The ISHRS is an organization that helps hair restoration surgeons share research, resources, and connect with one another, as well a resource for patients to find information on hair-loss and restoration treatment options.

Finding accurate and truthful medical information online is harder than it should be. To have information like this easily available to users presents the ISHRS as an organization with integreity and a focus on their patients. The website also serves as a resource for hair restoration surgeons, and our client wanted us to increase website engagement by both patients and doctors.

I researched what patients will want do first when they land on a health website. I made a "Find a Doctor" call to action so users can find the nearest ISHRS approved doctor, and seperated medical facts for women and men instead of having them on one page.

By optimizing the UI and IA for their website, traffic increased by over 22% in one year.
