MyBeesbox

Role: UI Designer & Developer

Software: WordPress w/Elementor & WooCommerce

Results

  • $64,000 its first month in business
  • Had an average click conversion rate of 2.1% throughout it first year of launch.

MyBeesbox is a Midwestern based online salesplace that sells handmade and unique gifts from artists and craftspeople from across the United States.

My client came to me with an idea, but nothing else. I was given an index card's worth of ideas as to build from.

I interviewed my client with empathy, conducted research on what inspired them and their competetors' strategies. I provided wireframes, user research, and prototypes before delivering the final product.

MyBeesbox made $64,000 in its first month of businesss, and maintained an average click conversion rate of 2.1% throughout it first year of launch.
